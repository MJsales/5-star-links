Questions

Do I need to take new photos?

No. That's the whole point. We pull from the photos already on your listing. If you happen to have higher-resolution originals sitting on your phone or camera, email them over and we'll use those since they give a sharper final video — but it's completely optional.

What exactly do I get?

A finished MP4 video file, sized for wherever you plan to post it. Airbnb itself doesn't accept video uploads on most listings, so the real value is Instagram Reels, TikTok, Facebook, your direct-booking site, and anywhere you advertise the property. Pro and Portfolio include multiple sizes so one order covers every platform.

How fast is delivery?

48 hours for Starter, 24 hours for Pro and Portfolio, counted from when you approve the plan. If you're up against a deadline, say so in the notes and we'll tell you honestly whether we can make it.

What if I don't like it?

Every package includes revisions — one for Starter, two for Pro, unlimited for Portfolio. Tell us what feels off and we recut it. You don't pay anything until you've approved the plan for the video.

Is the music actually cleared?

Yes. We only use licensed or royalty-free tracks, so your post won't get muted or hit with a claim on Instagram or TikTok.

I manage several properties. Can I get a deal?

Portfolio covers up to 3 listings at $299. If you run more than that, send the request form with "Multiple Properties" selected and we'll put together a per-unit rate for you.
